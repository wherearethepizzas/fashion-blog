An Insider's Guide to NYFW

NYFW can be both amazingly fun and incredibly overwhelming, especially if you've never been there.
Luckily, I'm here to give you an Insider's guide and make your first experience. By taking my tips and tricks borrowed from reknown celebs like Kendall Jenner & Gigi Hadid and following your gut, you'll have an unforgettable experience!

Getting Tickets & Picking Shows

If you're lucky or connected you can get an invite, says the price tag. Although i wasn't so lucky or connected my first 2 years so i'm here to help you out. First, plan out which shows are most important to you and make a schedule and this is a must: SET A BUDGET. I know you're worrying about blowing your cash the whole time and that you wont have fun otherwise. Don't worry, Check out the prices, days and times here How to Attend or Buy Tickets.
Prioritize the designers you want to see most and last but not least, purchase your tickets and get excited!

Dressing for the Shows


Always be true to your own sense of style, if you don't you'll be uncomfortable the whole time and taking in what the designers have chosen to express through their new lines.also it's important to wear shoes you'll be comfortable in all day. Obviously you wanna look good, but you'll be on your feet all day so be prepared.
